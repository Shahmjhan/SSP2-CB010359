  • Do you have minimum quantities per order?

    We are one the few uniform businesses that allow you to order as many or as little as you want! The problem with uniform companies that have a "Minimum Policy" is that you order your bulk range which is fine.
    But then you want to order one or two new garments for a new staff member and they make you buy 50 units. That will never happen here with our apparel range.

  • How much does the freight cost?

    Our minimum freight & handling charge for the first label is only 1995.00/= (exc tax).
    This means that if you wishes to buy products from additional labels, then you will only be charged an additional 995/= per label thereafter (exc tax).

  • How long does it take to receive my order?

    Unfortunaltey we can not give definate delivery times as that is the responsibility of the out-sourced freight company and the location to where it is being delivered.

  • Can we fax or telephone our order to you?

    As we are an online business, we are doing everything to keep costs down so that we can pass on the savings to you! One of the most expensive components of a uniform business is when staff need to manually enter orders.
