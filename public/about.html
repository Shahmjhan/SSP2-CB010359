Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

Nine years down the road, countless discussions revolving around uniforms, a memorable backpacking adventure, a joyous wedding celebration, and numerous sourcing trips to Italy later, the dream of owning a uniform shop materialized. It was in 2004 that Jhans inaugurated Uniform Studio within Sri Lanka’s bustling Badulla’ Arcade. Jhans relinquished his role as a marketing executive to spearhead the business venture, Their inventory boasted exquisitely crafted uniforms catering to both genders, a venture that proved highly successful – within two years, Jhans transitioned into the business full-time, and they swiftly expanded, establishing three additional brick-and-mortar stores along with an online presence within a span of four years. The retail uniform landscape was thriving, yet corporate uniforms languished in mediocrity. Off-the-shelf solutions and bulk orders resulted in ill-fitting, uninspired attire. Recognizing the pivotal role uniforms played in brand representation, Jhans discerned yet another niche in the market, embarking on the journey to offer bespoke uniforms.

The Uniform Edit


Life is a journey marked by constant evolution. As our focus transitioned from shirts to encompass complete uniforms, it became evident that a name change was necessary. Thus, in the early months of 2019, we embarked on a gentle transition, bidding farewell to the 15-year legacy of Shirt Studio Corporate and embracing the boundless opportunities that The Uniform Edit promises for both our team and our clients. Amidst the flux of change, one steadfast element remains untouched – our unwavering commitment. At the helm of our enterprise are our founders, Jhans and Fiona Anchal, whose dedication knows no bounds. Their fervor for delivering excellence to the Australian market and collaborating with clients to actualize their visions remains as fervent as ever. Bolstered by a team equally devoted, seasoned, and adept, The Uniform Edit stands as an indomitable force, poised to conquer new horizons.
Since 2001, The Uniform Store has been a steadfast presence in the Badulla region, offering top-notch uniforms to various sectors. Our commitment to excellence spans across healthcare, hospitality, and office environments, catering to small, medium, and large businesses alike. Under current ownership since 2009, the business has thrived, expanding its footprint and tripling revenue through positive word-of-mouth referrals. Our diverse product range includes medical uniforms, lab coats, hospitality attire, slip-resistant shoes, casual office wear, and a wide array of uniform accessories. For commercial clients, we boast access to an extensive network of uniform manufacturers, ensuring options for various occupational fields, including foodservice prep and manufacturing, with options even meeting ANSI and OSHA standards. Partnering with a local, family-owned embroidery business, we offer personalization and branding services, elevating your company's image. Moreover, we take pride in our social responsibility initiatives, actively participating in the Free The Girls campaign to support former human trafficking victims in Mozambique. With our unwavering dedication, adaptability, client-centric approach, and commitment to quality, The Uniform Store stands as the premier destination for all your uniform needs.
